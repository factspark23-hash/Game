HOW TO PLAY

🧠 The Concept

MIND is an 18-level brain training gauntlet that tests every cognitive function simultaneously. Memory, logic, pattern recognition, reaction speed, spatial reasoning, and math — all at once.

⚔️ Levels 1-10: Foundation

1. Neural Storm — Memorize a grid, then fill missing cells using row/column math constraints.

2. Dual Cortex — Remember color AND tone sequences simultaneously. Reproduce both.

3. Logic Web — Solve interconnected logic puzzles with unique constraints.

4. Spatial Hell — Mental rotation under time pressure. Identify rotated shapes.

5. Memory Overload — Memorize an entire symbol grid, answer detailed questions.

6. Stroop Inferno — Name the COLOR of text, not what it says. Brain interference.

7. Attention Split — Track a moving dot AND solve math. Both must be correct.

8. Pattern Maze — Dual number sequences with compound hidden rules.

9. Synapse Fire — 4 sub-problems (memory + logic + math + counting) combined into one answer.

10. Omega Mind — All skills combined in a 4-phase gauntlet.

🔥 Levels 11-15: Combat

11. Reaction Storm — Click targets before they escape. Pure speed. Hit 20 targets.

12. Pattern Combat — Find the odd shape among 4. Lightning fast.

13. Speed Demon — Dodge 🔴 obstacles, grab 🟢 points. Items accelerate. Quick math bonuses.

14. Reflex Duel — Combat signals vs decoys. HIT ⚔️ or SKIP 🌸 in milliseconds.

15. Final Boss — 3 mini-challenges per round. Reaction + Pattern + Math + Stroop + Memory combos.

🧠 Levels 16-18: Prediction

16. Bounce Predict — Watch a ball bounce with physics. Predict where it will land. Click to place your guess.

17. Sequence Oracle — Observe patterns in colors, numbers, positions, rotations. Predict what comes next.

18. Mind Reader — Complex multi-element interactions. Collisions, distributions, chain reactions. Think like the system.

⚡ Scoring

Combo System: Consecutive correct answers build a combo multiplier (x2, x3, x4, x5). Wrong answers reset it.

Lives: You get 3 lives. Lose all = restart. Retrying costs a life.

Leaderboard: Your best runs are saved locally. Beat your own records.

🎯 Tips

• Stay calm. Panicking makes you slower.

• Trust your first instinct on reaction tests.

• For memory levels, use chunking — group items mentally.

• On Stroop levels, say the COLOR out loud to override reading.

• Speed levels: anticipate. Don't react — predict.
